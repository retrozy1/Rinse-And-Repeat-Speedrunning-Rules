**----Timing----**

The run starts when you load into the 3D gameplay.

Full-Game runs end when the text says that all endings for the respective category have been completed after completing the endings.

Level runs end when the ending message pops up.

**----General Rules----**

If your run is **below 30 fps** it will be rejected, due to retiming issues. Your run must be consistently 30 fps or higher. Top 3 runs in categories/levels that have over 3 runs must at least close to 60 fps (not for legitimacy issues but for retiming issues.) If the category/level has 3 runs or less, than this onlsy applies to the world record.

You must show at least the frame that the run starts and at least the frame the category would end.

Full-Game runs must be shorter than 10 minutes. Level runs must be shorter than 5 minutes.

No auto clickers are allowed.

**----Unrequired/Allows----**

Runs can be reused. For example, you can reuse a run from All Endings for every category and level.

No game audio or input audio is required, but is appreciated.

A timer is not required but is appreciated.
